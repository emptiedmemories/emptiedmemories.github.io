導演／概念 ｜ 周東彥
Director & Concept ｜ Chou, Tung-Yen

－ 倫敦中央聖馬丁藝術暨設計學院，劇場與多媒體碩士 （MA Scenography）
－ 國立台北藝術大學戲劇學系畢業，主修導演

Chou, Tung-Yen holds a MA in Scenography with distinction from Central Saint Martins College of Art and Design in London and a BFA in Theatre Directing from TNUA. He is the founder and director of Very Mainstream Studio, and now the lecturer in school of Theatre Arts, TNUA. Besides working on film & theatre pieces that are performed and screened internationally, he also dedicates to the realm of technology & performing arts.

現為狠主流多媒體負責人，並於北藝大戲劇學系擔任講師，曾任國立政治大學傳播學院業界專業教師。創作以影像及劇場為核心，影像作品四度入圍台北電影獎，近年亦致力於科技表演藝術跨域創作，成績斐然，導演作品《寫給記憶的七封信》（2008）、《空的記憶》（2011-2012）皆備受矚目。

編舞／演出 ｜ 周書毅
Choreographer & Performer ｜ Chou, Shu-Yi

－ 新世代倍受矚目的新銳編舞與表演家。

The new promising and talented choreographer and performer.
Chou Shu-Yi was born in 1983. He started his career as an independent choreographer. from 2003. His works mainly focus on the physical movement of a dancing body, while he never stops challenging the pre-existing artistic structure to explore a cross-genre performance style. His recent productions include Start with the Body, 1875 Ravel and Bolero, S, and etc. Acknowledged by the international dance society, Shu-Yi Chou has won several performing art awards.  Throughout his artistic practice, his choreographic works have truthfully represented his life, his work, the journey he has taken, and the sensitive mind which accompanies the artist when he is away from home. Through dance, he expresses his true self. In the year of 2011, Shu-yi & Dancers formally starts a collative project with dancers as well as the artists from various genres.

2003年開始獨立編舞的創作生涯，透過舞蹈直敘生活、社會、旅行及流浪時，所看見的情感，提出自己與環境的表演形貌「屬於我們的身體語彙」。曾赴紐約及巴黎等地進行駐村創作，作品亦多次擄獲國際舞蹈大賽肯定。2009年作品《1875‧拉威爾與波麗露》獲得英國表演藝術殿堂「沙德勒之井」劇院第一屆全球網路影片比賽首獎Sadler’s WellGlobal Dance Contest，2010年一月受邀於倫敦該劇院演出2場，同年十月獲紐約「秋季舞蹈藝術節」Fall For Dance Festival演出邀約。

同年《從身體出發》榮獲丹麥Cross connection ballet編舞大賽銅牌。2011年成立【周先生】團隊，正式開啟與舞者們和不同領域的藝術家的未來計畫。2012年受邀第40屆香港藝術節亞太舞蹈平台，發表全新獨舞《關於活著這一件事》。

合作團隊

Produced by Very Mainstream Studio
製作團隊：狠主流多媒體有限公司

Founded by theater & media artist Chou, Tung-Yen, VM studio creates works at multi-media design, film, documentary, experimental short film, installations. It also engages in the field of technology & performing arts in recent years. VM studio has got sponsored and awarded several times, and performed internationally. It has collaborated with: Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Dept. of Cultural Affairs, Taipei, Eslite Bookstore, Taishin Bank Foundation for Arts and Culture, Quanta Arts Foundation, Performance Workshop, Taiwan Drama Performance, Flying-group Theatre, Mr. Wing Theatre Company, Taipei Chinese Orchestra, Dark Eyes Performance Lab, etc.. 

為劇場與影像創作者周東彥創立，一個年輕而有活力的多媒體製作公司。名為｢狠主流｣意即希望在創作的實踐上，超越主流與非主流、文化與商業、藝術與娛樂的界限，讓主流可不斷再度被定義與挑戰。  狠主流以影像創作為核心，由此延伸出多元的設計與藝術創作相關業務，把對影像與劇場的熱誠轉化為實際專業的經營理念，秉持著藝術工作者敏銳而獨到的堅持，期許未來能全方位的遊走在各創作領域，帶給觀者更多的想像空間與情感共鳴。  在影音製作上，除了多部短片受到國內外電影節的邀約、入圍等肯定。也因著藝術創作背景，廣受藝文團體、基金會、藝術家之邀，拍攝多部以文化藝術創作為主題的紀錄片、創作影片及宣傳片等。  曾經合作單位包括：外交部、台北市文化局、高雄市文化局、誠品書店、台新銀行文化藝術基金會、廣藝基金會、中強光電文化藝術基金會、表演工作坊、臺灣戲劇 表演家、莎士比亞的妹妹們的劇團、台南人劇團、飛人集社、人力飛行劇團、周先生與舞者們、九歌民族管弦樂團、白雪綜藝劇團、NSO、臺北市立國樂團、黑眼 睛跨劇團、BENQ、LG、Amadeus......等。

  • 行政統籌 ｜國立臺北藝術大學北藝風創新育成中心
    Administrative Coordinator ｜KD Art Culture Industry Innovation Incubation Center

    Since its establishment TNUA has been keen on producing talented artists. However, in the course of its educational work TNUA has realized that many talented young students will be left without resources or support to continue working in art-related fields after leaving school. Or they will have to spend extra time or efforts to pursue their goals or to adapt to new environments. Therefore, TNUA started contemplating an assistance mechanism within the framework of art.
    In September 2008, with full backing from the Council for Cultural Affairs, TNUA decided to set up the Kd Arts Culture Industry Innovation Incubation Center, seeking to provide long-term and systematic support to help young artists realize their dream of creating a business of the arts. The Center hopes to play a “last mile” role after the arts students leave school and enter society, providing them with professional resources from TNUA and a place where young artists can explore business opportunities.

    作為北藝大對外聯繫的重要窗口，除執行由文化部補助之育成中心業務外，亦擴大「北藝風」品牌之經營管理。另肩負學校整體行銷功能與業務，並開發各種創意產業以及藝企合作的可能性。近年規劃並承辦多項校內外展演活動，例如：2008-2012關渡藝術節行銷媒體公關規劃執行、2010廣藝科技藝術節、2011年外交部春宴、2012廣藝BRAVO兩岸！青世代劇展˙北京篇、2012財團法人果實文教基金會「聽我說故事創作營」等，成功為新銳藝術家開展演出平台，以及跨領域展演的實踐。
